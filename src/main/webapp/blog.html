SEO Company BrightEdge unveiled new research findings on the performance of Organic and Paid Search as a marketing channel. The strength of search marketing - Organic Search and Paid Search - is clear. Organic Search remains the dominant source of trackable web traffic and in the dominant position as a channel. Paid Search continues to grow. Organic Social Media is flat since 2014 at 5%, and though ubiquitous, contributes on average 1/3 as much traffic as Paid Search and just 1/11 as much as Organic Search.
